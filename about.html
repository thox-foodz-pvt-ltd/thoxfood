OUR VISION
We all love home cooked food, don’t we! In our day to day lives stuck up in office and travel, we find it hard to manage the time for cooking. That is where we -Thox want to be the One Stop Solution that provides you ‘Maa-k- hath ka khaana.’ We even want to empower women by giving them the opportunity to work from home and get paid for what they do best- Cooking!



OUR SOLUTION
We provide food in fairly reasonable rates and because the housewives are making food at their home with the best quality of ingredients we have passed to use gives us an edge in terms of food quality.

OUR JOURNEY

Once a student, then an employee. In the process of it, is when the journey started for the Founder – Abhishek Tyagi! As a college student with big aspirations, he once decided to drop out of college and pursue a corporate job in BPO sector. Days passed and he became a regular employee until the very day, when his parents asked him to live on his own, to which he agreed and moved out to live a brand new life of freedom. health of uncountable number of people!
